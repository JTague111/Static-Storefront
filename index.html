About Us

Sunspot Gaming is Indy's premier open air gaming arena. Founded in 2021 we aimed to create a healthy and pleasent gmaing environment for both casual and competitive players of board games and TCGs. We are solely a venue for tournaments and casual gaming, and for products are partnered with the nearby game store nightfall games. We hope to see you soon for one of our nightly events
